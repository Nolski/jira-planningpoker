TVTA-9012 - Report Query Service

AS AN Avoca System I WANT TO provide all authenticated users complimentary access to an Avoca Subscription SO THAT I can gain a user base and demonstrate the value of an Avoca Subscription.

Associated Business Requirements
1. Avoca shall provide all Authenticated Users complimentary access to all functionality permissible by the Avoca Subscription.
2. For users who already have an Avoca account, Avoca shall provide a complimentary Avoca trial subscription beginning July 1, 2013 and ending July 31, 2013.
3. For users who create an account on or after July 1, 2013, Avoca shall provide a complimentary Avoca trial subscription beginning the day they register and expiring 30 days thereafter.
4. Avoca shall require users purchase an Avoca Subscription to have access to the functionality permissible by the Avoca Subscription upon expiration of the complimentary trial period.
5. If a user purchases an Avoca Subscription prior to the expiration of their 30 day trial, Avoca shall automatically begin their Avoca Subscription (e.g., X number of days left on trial - > user purchases an Avoca Subscription, Avoca Subscription begins immediately and user does not receive additional X number of days left on trial).

Acceptance Criteria - Users with an Avoca Account Prior to July 1
1. I create an Avoca account prior to July 1.
2. On July 1 I begin my complimentary trial period.
3. I have access to all functionality and content for the duration of the complimentary trial period (so long as I'm authenticated).
4. The last day of my trial period is July 31. If I do not purchase a subscription I will no longer have access to the functionality permissible by the Avoca Subscription.

Acceptance Criteria - Users who create an Avoca Account On or After July 1
1. It is July 15 and I create an Avoca Account.
2. On July 15 I begin my complimentary trial period.
3. I have access to all functionality and content for the duration of the complimentary trial period (so long as I'm authenticated).
4. The last day of my trial period is thirty days after July 15. If I do not purchase a subscription upon expiration of the trial period I will no longer have access to the functionality permissible by the Avoca Subscription.
